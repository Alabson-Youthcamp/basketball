ABOUT US

The Timion Alabson Basketball Youth Camp is a Corporate Social Responsibility Initiative designed to engage youths within communities between the ages of twelve and eighteen years, in constructive development by the means of a basketball training camp where they are able to channel their energies positively, keeping them away from social vices and building an interest in a sportsman's means of livelihood.
